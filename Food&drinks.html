Food and drink

Enjoy the heart of Bergen

Breakfast, lunch and dinner We serve both continental breakfasts and modern asian cuisine.

Regarded by critics as one of the best Chinese restaurants in Norway, Soya Restaurant serves both continental breakfast for our guests and delicious modern Asian cuisine. Wake up to freshly brewed coffee and freshly baked bread; in the evening – enjoy authentic Chinese Dim Sum.

Breakfast hours

Monday – Friday: 07:00 – 09:30

Saturday – Sunday: 07:30 – 10:30

Experience the best Italy has to offer; homemade Italian pizza and some carefully selected Italian dishes. With us, everything is ready for an authentic Italian dining experience and a good atmosphere in pleasant surroundings. Our goal is to serve the city’s best pizza. Buon appetito!

Make a reservation at Porto13

Belgio is Bergen’s first – and only – Belgian bar. The breadth and diversity of Belgian beer culture has led to it being placed on UNESCO’s cultural heritage list. We have a wide selection and serve everything from tart Lambic beers to the more mellow blond ales.

Opening hours

Monday – Friday: 07:00 – 09:30

Saturday – Sunday: 07:30 – 10:30

Welcome to the jungle! Just above Harbour Cafe you’ll find the lively Tonga Bar. With interiors made from real bamboo, Tonga offers a unique atmosphere and Bergen’s widest selection of exotic cocktail drinks.

Opening hours

Monday – Friday: 07:00 – 09:30

Saturday – Sunday: 07:30 – 10:30

Since 1995 Scruffy Murphy’s has been Bergen’s foremost Irish pub. Here you will find live music performed by professional musicians, football matches and a fantastic atmosphere. Neighbouring the pub, you’ll also find Scruffy’s Dinner which serve delicious Irish pub dishes.

Opening hours

Monday – Friday: 07:00 – 09:30

Saturday – Sunday: 07:30 – 10:30

Metz Bar is a sports bar by the Fish Market that transforms into a pulsating night club by night with DJs playing your favorite music from the 80s to today.

Opening hours

Monday – Friday: 07:00 – 09:30

Saturday – Sunday: 07:30 – 10:30

Sunday: 12:00 – 01:30

Michelsen is a vibrant cafe overlooking the octagonal lake of Lille Lungegårdsvannet. At Michelsen you can enjoy delicious food and drinks paired with one of the best views in the city!

Opening hours

Monday – Friday: 07:00 – 09:30
